Create: Techified Lite


About

Create: Techified Lite is a trimmed down version of the Create: Techified modpack.


Features

1. Optimized Performance: With actually less performance mods, this version mystically runs better!

2. Same Endgame Experience: All of Techified, without most of the fluff.

3. Server-Friendly: Made specifically for a scuffed server, but works great for flying solo too.

4. Custom Content: Mostly the same content, just tweaked to work with the limited mod availability.

 

If you were wanting to play Techified, but your computer is from the stone age, this version.. might help.
