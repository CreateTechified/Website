PROJECT Odysseus


About

While going through space time, an unknown, strong gravity force causes your space ship to crash into a planet, where quantum energy took over everything. No life, no breathable air, nothing.
It is YOUR mission to revive this planet.

 

{PROJECT ODYSSEUS} is a sequel to Create Techified, where the leftover quantum energy seeped through the cracks of the fabric of reality and killing everything in its way, the soil, animals, plants, even water is not left

 

With the power of Spatial Technology and other assets, the fate of the planet is in your hands.
