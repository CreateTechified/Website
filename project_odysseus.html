PROJECT Odysseus


About

PROJECT Odysseus is a sequel to Create Techified, where excess quantum energy has ended all life on the planet. It's YOUR mission to bring life back to the planet and save the day.


Features

1. Post-Apocalyptic World: A planet ravaged by quantum energy - no life, no breathable air, not even water remains. The fate of the planet rests in your hands.

2. Reclaim the Planet: Master Spatial Technology and advanced tools to restore MASSAGE-2(A-B)b to its former glory.

3. Sequel Experience: Continue after the events of Create: Techified, where the past pioneers left a bit of a mess for you to clean up.


If you played Create: Techified and Satisfactory, and really liked the idea of a story factory-building game, I recommend you give PROJECT Odysseus a try!
