PROJECT Evolution


About

Ever wanted to see how humans evolved all the way to today?
{PROJECT EVOLUTION} brings a lot of mods together to offer you the best experience,
A slow but lore accurate progression from Stone Age to Quantum Technology, you name it!
As you progress you can also document the new things you discover, from elements to species.

 

Features

1. Optional Quests: Feeling stuck? The Quest Book is here to help!

2. Addition to the Lore accurate Progression: From Primitive Tools to Machines that break the laws of reality, we've made it so that you progress further than humans have ever gone

3. Many new things to uncover: Discover over [-] new elements, compounds and materials

4. A Challenging World: The difficulty increases as you progress!
