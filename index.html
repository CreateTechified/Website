Create: Techified

About

Create Techified is a collection of many great mods and implements them with Create in some way, compiled into an epic journey towards Quantum Advancement.
With many factories lying between You and The Quantum Realm, it's your mission to make the most out of your factories.
Tired of staying in the industrial environment? Fear not! The world offers many adventures for you.

 

Features

1. Storage Options: Storing all your messy items is easier than ever. From Applied Energistics 2 to Refined Storage to ...Create? The choice is yours

2. Optional Quests: Got stuck? The Quest Book got you covered! Ready to guide you through your journey

3. Tinker's Integration: The early game is very modified, making tools is now more unique than ever thanks to Tinker's Construct.

4. Upgrade your Factories while you progress: While progressing through the modpack, there are many opportunities to upgrade your factories with new machines and more!

 

So, if you were looking for an expert create modpack, this might just be for you!
